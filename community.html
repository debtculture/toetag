Leaderboard: How It Works

Earning Points

Points are awarded for participation and top performance in official weekly community events like Trivia and Poker.

  • 1st Place: 10 Points
  • 2nd Place: 7 Points
  • 3rd Place: 5 Points
  • Participation: 1 Point

Monthly Payouts

The "Monthly" leaderboard resets at the beginning of each month. Payouts for the previous month's final standings will be distributed within the first three days of the new month.

  • 🥇 1st Place: 100,000 $DEBT
  • 🥈 2nd Place: 50,000 $DEBT
  • 🥉 3rd Place: 25,000 $DEBT

Yearly & All-Time

The "Yearly" and "All-Time" tabs track your long-term performance and legacy within the community. A special "Player of the Year" prize will be introduced for the 2026 season.
